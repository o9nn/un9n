- Truth-conditional semantics

**2. Procedural Knowing (Knowing-How)**

- Implicit know-how through embodied practice
- Pattern recognition through experience

**3. Perspectival Knowing (Knowing-As)**

**4. Participatory Knowing (Knowing-By-Being)**

- Agapic love and deep connection

**Integration Principle:** These ways work together in integrated fashion. Wisdom requires harmonious synthesis, not reduction to propositional knowledge alone.

- **Filters** - Reduce overwhelming complexity

Constantly balancing tradeoffs:

**No Meta-Criterion:** There is no algorithm for determining relevance in general. Relevance realization is itself an optimization process requiring wisdom.
